About Me

Chris Eure is a composer and audio producer. Interested in music for film, Eure's musical influences includes Beethoven, Shostakovich, Shoenberg, Bernard Hermann and Metallica. His primary genres of interest are orchestral metal as well as horror. In addition to music, Eure is also interested in technology and how to bridge the two in composition and other audio production. Chris lives in Drexel Hill with his wife and two year old son.
